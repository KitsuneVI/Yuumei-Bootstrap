Hello! I'm Yuumei, an illustrator, designer, and comic artist. I've been sharing my Art & Comics online for free all my life. From short comics like 1000 Words to my recent Cyberpunk series, Fisheye Placebo, I love visual storytelling to convey the more intricate and complicated conflicts in our world.

Please help keep my website free!

I hope you enjoy the artworks, comics, and tutorials on here. I aim to keep this site ad free, and all content on here free as well. I don't like the idea of making aspiring artists pay to have access to my tutorials, or making kids pay for reading my comics, so everything on this site is always free. If you have the money to spare, and would like to see more work from me, please donate to support my career as an artist.

When you donate, you can choose from the checkbox which part of the site you would like to see more off. Be it more comics, tutorials, or artworks in general, let me know what you like best :)

Alternatively, you can purchase from my shop. or become patron on Patreon. I also donate 10% of all shop proceeds to environmental charities :)
